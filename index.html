Country Animal Clinic has been owned and operated by Dr. Gene Metzger since 1997.

Dr. Gene is available for office calls Mondays, Tuesdays, Fridays and Saturdays.

Dr. Marcie Locke is available on Wednesdays, Thursdays, Fridays, and Saturdays.

Office Calls are by appointment, please contact our office to set up a time to have your pet evaluated.

We ask that you arrive 10 minutes early for your appointment. Please be sure to bring proof of your animals vaccinations. All pets seen in our clinic are required to have the proper vaccinations for your pets health, and the safety of our staff.

If you are unable to make your appointment on time, please contact our office.
